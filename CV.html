Independent SAGE

WIESS

Research assistant

I worked with Sophia Bano at WIESS on TTTS image segmentation using CNNs- creating the segmentation model and labelling the data and co authoring ‘Deep Placental Vessel Segmentation for Fetoscopic Mosaicking’

Vision and Imaging Science Group

Research assistant

I worked for Marta Betke to create a python package to manipulate voxel and STL ankle bone reconstructions from sparse angle CT scans which is included in ‘Using segmented CBCT scans to aid reconstruction of dynamic scan of a rigid foot and ankle structure’

Principles and Practice of Remote Sensing

Teaching assistant

Conflict Analysis Lab

Research assistant

Thinking Deep expedition

Safety diver
